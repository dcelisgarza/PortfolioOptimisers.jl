This pass hunted re-encodings, drifted copies, and change amplification left after the ADR-driven consolidations (factory/view macros, JuMP model assembly, SmythBroby kernel, scalarise seam, NormError, MIP space). Much of the library already has single points of change — drawdown builders share set_drawdown_constraints!, Black-Litterman priors share bl_preroll, windowed moments share windowed_preamble, tests share per-suite setup files, and there is no load-time @eval codegen. What remains clusters in two hot spots — the XatRisk Range family and the uncertainty-set constructors — plus a handful of idiom-level re-encodings. Two candidates carry confirmed drift, one with a live behavioural divergence.

Problem. Every Range measure is definitionally the base measure applied to x and −x, yet the family encodes that twice per site and in two styles: EVaR/RVaR/PNVaR ranges delegate to the ERM/RRM/PRM kernels (08:277, 09:385, 19:325), while the CVaR and VaR ranges re-encode their base functor inline — the loss half of RMCVaRRg{Nothing} is a line-for-line copy of RMCVaR{Nothing}, and drift is already visible: the GenericValueatRiskRange docstring union (21:34–41) omits WorstRealisation, which the real constructor accepts (21:129–140).   • one delegation style, not two
  • docstring drift becomes impossible
  • new tail measure gets Range free

Problem. The library's docstring convention hand-copies each struct's full kwarg signature into a "# Constructors" block and its argument checks into a "## Validation" list — so every signature lives in three copies (docstring, inner constructor, outer constructor) held in sync only by discipline. It is a reflection contract with no enforcement: the GenericValueatRiskRange docstring already lags its constructor by one accepted type. field_dict/arg_dict/math_dict consolidated the prose fragments; signatures are the remaining hand-copy.

Direction. A doc-build check (or interpolated fragment) that derives the "# Constructors" block from the outer method's actual signature, turning signature drift into a CI failure instead of stale docs.

  • 3 signature copies → 1 + derived
  • drift becomes a CI failure
  • ~170 files covered by one check
